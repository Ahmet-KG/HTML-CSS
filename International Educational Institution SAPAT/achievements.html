Our Achievements


International Olympiad “Genius”


On June 17-22, 2019, the International Genius Olympiad was held in the USA, where 1400 students from 78 countries of the world and 39 states of America competed in 5 different categories.
A team of pupils from the Municipal Educational Institution “Sapat” won 2 gold, 5 silver, 5 bronze medals and 13 incentive prizes. Congratulations to the winners!

Highest scores in ORT


18 students of the International Educational Institution “Sapat” received the highest scores on ORT results.
Among educational institutions, the largest number of gold certificates on the account of our lyceums. Congratulations to our students and teachers, we wish you great success!
